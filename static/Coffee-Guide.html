How to Make a Latte

Breville Oracle Jet Touch

Press Start to Begin
Step 1 of 6

Prepare

Fill milk jug with fresh cold milk to just below the spout mark. Place jug under steam wand.

Image / Video
Step 2 of 6

Select Milk Type

Tap the milk icon on screen. Choose your milk type: Dairy, Almond, Soy, or Oat.

Image / Video
Step 3 of 6

Steam the Milk

Press down the steam lever. The screen displays temperature in real time. Machine stops automatically at your set temperature.

Image / Video
Step 4 of 6

Insert Portafilter

Insert the portafilter into the group head. Press to lock. The machine will automatically grind, dose, and tamp.

Image / Video
Step 5 of 6

Brew Espresso

Tap the espresso icon. Machine extracts a double shot of espresso directly into your cup below the portafilter.

Image / Video
Step 6 of 6

Pour & Enjoy

Pour the steamed milk into the espresso cup. Top with a small layer of microfoam. Your latte is ready!

Image / Video

Enjoy Your Latte

Well Done!
