How to Make a Coffee

Follow the steps on the Oracle Jet Touch

Step 1 of 7

Equipment list

You will use: mug, portafilter, filter basket, milk jug, knock box and the Oracle Jet Touch screen.

Make sure all items are within easy reach before you begin.

All equipment layout
Step 2 of 7

Preparation

Check the bench is clear and dry and the machine is on and warmed up.

Place a clean mug under the group head, then touch the drink icon on the screen to choose your coffee.

Machine ready / drink selection
Step 3 of 7

Grind coffee

Insert the empty portafilter into the left grind position and lock it in place.

When it is seated, touch the portafilter icon on the left to grind, dose and tamp automatically.

Left icon / Portafilter
Step 4 of 7

Brewing

Move the prepared portafilter to the middle group head and lock it firmly.

Check the mug is centred, then touch the middle brew icon to start extraction.

Middle icon / Brew
Step 5 of 7

Milk

Fill the milk jug with cold milk to just below the spout line.

Place the jug under the steam wand, then touch the milk icon on the right to steam automatically.

Right icon / Milk
Step 6 of 7

Pour milk

Slowly pour the steamed milk into the espresso in your mug.

When the cup is full, set the jug down and enjoy your coffee.

Pouring milk into espresso
Step 7 of 7

Cleanup

Remove the portafilter and knock the used puck into the knock box.

Rinse the portafilter, basket and milk jug, wipe them dry, and place everything back ready for the next person.

Knock box / Rinse & ready
Press Restart to Start Over
